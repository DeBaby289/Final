Animal Rescue

Animal rescue is a game raising awareness to people about all the harsh things being done to animals. In this game we have two main characters named Chipotle and Squirt. Chipotle and Squirt are on two different levels and each level is telling a different story. In Chipotles story it's letting people know how many cats/kittens are being abused in their homes and how their being treated differently and not being cared for. Squirts story is letting people know how elephants around the world are being beaten in circuses in Africa and Asia and squirt is also trying to help elephants who are being hunted by local poachers in Africa.

Squirt

This is Squirt, Squirt is a one year old baby elephant who trying to escape local poachers and trying to rescue other elephants from those poachers. Squirt is also trying to save other elephants who are being abused in circuses. Squirts main purpose is to help elephants find loving people that will treat and care for them in a loving way.Squirt is going around the world to find friendly elephants who are being hunted, abused, and tortured.

Chipotle

Chipotle is a 3 month old kitten who has escaped her abusive home in the search of a better home. In her journey, she helps other cats escape their abusive homes and find new homes throughout their journey.
